Who am I ?

Hello ! My name is Karine. I am a freelance ghostwriter and proofreader based in Madagascar. But I also am a literature and film enthusiast who will love to help you on your next project. A book, a film, a series, you name it !
Throughout my years on the job market, I found a passion in helping and assisting others to bring their visions to life. I love the process of creating a story, from the worldbuilding to the characters and the plot scheming, but most importantly, I want to bring emotions into the public.
Having a spotlight is not what I look for. I like to work in the shadows. My goal is simply to be part of something greater than me. Maybe your project ! *wink*
